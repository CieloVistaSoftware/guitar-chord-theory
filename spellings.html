Chord formulas

Every chord type is really just a formula of scale-degree numbers relative to the root — the same formula on any root gives you that same type of chord. Examples below are spelled in the selected key. Click any row to hear it.

Diatonic chords by key

The seven chords that come from harmonizing each major scale, labeled with Nashville Numbers. The pattern is always the same shape — major, minor, minor, major, major, minor, diminished — just starting from a different root. Click any chord to hear it.
